Professional Experience

Currently, I hold an internship at Sandia National Laboratories in their Autonomy for Hypersonics group. Previously, I've held an internship at Paul Mueller Company and was offered an internship that was later COVID-rescinded from Dolese Brothers.

Research Interests

I've been involved with the Lightwave Technology Laboratory under Dr. Jie Huang since Fall 2020. In this time, I've gained a wealth of experience working on several projects that utilize fiber optics and machine learning for sensing technologies.

Campus & Community Involvement

During my college career, I've been heavily involved in several organizations and community outreach projects. Whether it be as an Orientation Leader for Missouri S&T, the webmaster for the largest research center on campus, the president of my fraternity, or the community service chairman, I've always strived to gain a vast variety of experiences while promoting the community of Rolla.
